Common Questions

Student FAQs

Answers to what students ask most before and after enrolment.

How do I get access to the platform?

You apply through the Get Started page and your application is reviewed by an administrator. Once approved, you will receive your login credentials via email. Access is not automatic — it requires administrator confirmation.

Is there a timetable I must follow?

No. The platform is fully self-paced. You read your study materials whenever it suits you and sit your exam when you feel prepared. There are no mandatory sessions, no scheduled classes, and no live commitments.

Are there videos or audio recordings available?

No. The Professional Learning Hub is strictly a text-based reading and examination platform. There are no videos, no voice notes, and no live sessions. All learning materials are written documents uploaded by the administrator.

How will I know when my exam is available?

Your dashboard will display available exams as they are released by your administrator. You may also receive a notification via email. You can then log in and begin when you are ready.

What happens after I pass my exam?

Your results are reviewed by the administrator and a professional certificate is issued confirming your achievement. The certificate is a credible record of your successful completion of the programme.

Can I access the platform on my phone?

Yes. The platform is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. You can read your materials and complete your exams from any device with an internet connection.
